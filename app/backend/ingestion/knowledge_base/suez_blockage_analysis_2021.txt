Geopolitical Risk Analysis: 2021 Suez Canal Blockage

Event: The container ship "Ever Given" blocked the Suez Canal.
Duration: 6 days.

Primary Impacts:

Halted all traffic on the primary Asia-Europe shipping lane.

Global trade delayed by an estimated $9.6 billion per day.

East Coast US ports, especially Newark, saw major inventory shortages for EU-imported goods.

Rerouting around the Cape of Good Hope added 12-15 days to transit times.

Mitigation Strategy:

Companies with multi-source suppliers (e.g., from Turkey instead of just Asia) were less affected.

Increased holding of safety stock at key European hubs like Rotterdam.
